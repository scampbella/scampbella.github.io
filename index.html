About me

I'm an Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Oregon State University with a minor in Computer Science. I'm drawn to the intersection of software and hardware — building servers, writing tools, and figuring out how systems actually work under the hood.

Outside of classes I co-founded Oregon State's Computer Hardware Club, where we build multi-use servers and learn networking hands-on. I'm also a member of the App Development Club, collaborating on projects with Python, JavaScript, and various APIs.

When I'm not studying I'm usually assembling hardware, self-hosting services, or looking for a better way to do something that shouldn't be complicated.
